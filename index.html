  5. What has been your most notable achievement so far?
  6. Could you please let us know about an instance where you demonstrated leadership?

    7. How Do You Work?

  7. How do you preserve the information you obtain (E.g. Requirement from client, expectation from your manager)?
  8. How do you report back / update on progress?
  9. How do you prioritize your work?
  10. How do you deal with pressure at the work place?
  11. How do you handle criticism/negative feedback?
  12. How do you manage a situation where your co-workers are not working and its affecting your task & overall delivery of the project/sprint?
  13. Do you feel perfection is more important than meeting the deadline? Explain why.
  14. How do you ensure that the tasks assigned to you are completed successfully within the stipulated time?

    15. Learning & Development

  15. How do you measure your success (top 3 career metrics)?
  16. What are the key skills you need to possess to excel in your current role and how you are currently developing those skills or planning to develop those skills?
  17. How do you keep yourself updated with the latest technology stack?

Camera Presence Guidelines

  • Make sure you have a reliable internet connection and a quiet, distraction-free environment.
  • Be mindful of your camera angle and positioning. Sit up straight and look directly into the camera.
  • Stay in the middle of the frame
  • Use good lighting so your face is well-lit and easy to see.
  • Dress appropriately and professionally for the session.

Tone of Voice Guidelines

  • Speak clearly and at a moderate pace. Avoid speaking too quickly or too softly.
  • Be mindful of your tone of voice. Speak with confidence and avoid using filler words such as "um" or "like."
  • Avoid interrupting or talking over others during the interview.
  • Take notes as needed and be prepared to actively participate in the discussion.

Body Language Guidelines

  • Sit up straight and maintain good posture throughout the interview.
  • Don't fidget
  • Gesture and smile
